About Us

Co-founders and Design Principals, Ar. Gaurang Tajne and Ar. Rutuja Desai, lead co_LAB Design Studio, an innovative Interior and Architectural Design firm based in Mumbai.

With a practice rooted in simplicity, functionality, and thoughtful design, the studio specializes in High-End Residential Interiors, while also transforming commercial spaces, villas, and multi-functional facilities.
